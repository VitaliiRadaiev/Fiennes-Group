At Fiennes Restoration we have a team of some of the most skilled and talented coachbuilders you can hope to find who cover all areas of restoration.

The Fiennes Coachbuilding department is able to reproduce coachwork from drawings or photographs in the original style using the traditional coachbuilding skills and in general the coachwork designs are reproduced as they were originally conceived.

Our restoration skills cover coachwork, chassis restoration and straightening, refinishing and carpentry.
